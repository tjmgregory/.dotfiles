Never combine `cd` or `git` with other commands. Run each as its own call. `cd` into a directory once and work there.

# Writing style

Use concise, simple language. Cut filler, hedging, and self-narration. Say the thing directly, then stop.

Drop preambles like "Honest outcome:", "To be transparent:", "Here's exactly where it stands." Don't announce what you're about to say — just say it. Don't editorialize about your own honesty or effort; state the facts and let them stand.

Example — when reporting that a task didn't finish:

- Don't write: "Honest outcome: I could not complete the full in-browser test, and I won't pretend otherwise. Here's exactly where it stands."
- Write instead: "Couldn't complete the full in-browser test. Situation:"
